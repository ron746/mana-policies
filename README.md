## What this policy covers

RunMana is a running training app made by Mana Athletic LLC. It builds personalized 5K training plans, uses Apple Health to read your workouts and fitness signals, and runs on iPhone and Apple Watch.

This policy explains what data we collect, what we do with it, and the rights you have over your data.

**RunMana is a training app, not a medical device.** It is not a substitute for medical advice. Consult a physician before beginning any training program.

---

## Quick summary (the important parts)

- **Apple Health data stays on your device.** We read it; we never upload it.
- We only collect what we need to build and adapt your training plan.
- We never share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or anyone else.
- You can delete your data anytime.
- You can export your data anytime.
- We don't track you across other apps or websites.

The rest of this policy is the detailed version.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly

When you sign up and use RunMana, we collect:

- **Account information:** email address (or Apple Sign In token), name you choose to display
- **Profile information:** age, sex, school (if you're on a team), graduation year, days per week you can run, your weekly schedule preferences
- **Race calendar:** dates and ratings (A / B / C) of races you plan to enter
- **Feedback you give us:** subjective ratings on workouts (1–5 stars), notes you write, preferences you set
- **Race results:** times you report after races

### 1.2 Information from Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission (granted through Apple's standard HealthKit prompt), RunMana reads:

- Running workouts (distance, pace, duration)
- Heart rate during workouts
- VO₂max (Apple's estimate of your aerobic fitness)
- Heart rate recovery (one minute)
- Resting heart rate
- Active energy burned
- Running pace, speed, stride metrics
- GPS route data for workouts
- Your age and biological sex (used for fitness norm comparisons)

**Critical:** This Apple Health data stays on your iPhone. We read it, use it to build your plan, and discard the raw data. We do not upload your Apple Health data to our servers.

### 1.3 Information generated by RunMana

As you use the app, we generate:

- Your prescribed workouts (what we tell you to do today)
- Your completion status (did you do the workout? skip it? swap it?)
- Your Mana Mile fitness number (a derived metric, updated as you progress)
- Training load metrics (how much you've trained recently)
- The version of the methodology that generated your plan (for audit purposes)

This is the data we store on our servers (Supabase, in the United States).

## 2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

- **Build your training plan** — the core function of the app
- **Adapt your plan** when your fitness changes or you go off-plan
- **Show you your progress** — race projections, fitness trends, age-group comparisons
- **Send you the prescribed workout to your Apple Watch** (via Apple's WatchConnectivity)
- **Authenticate you** — confirm it's really you when you log in
- **Improve the app** — anonymous, aggregated usage signals only (no individual data)

We do **not** use your information to:

- Advertise to you
- Sell to data brokers
- Share with third parties for any purpose unrelated to providing you the service
- Make medical claims, diagnose conditions, or replace medical advice
- Train AI models on your individual data (any future research use would be opt-in, separately consented, and fully anonymized — see §8 below)

---

## 3. Apple HealthKit specifically (required disclosures per Apple)

Apple requires apps that use HealthKit to disclose specifically how that data is handled. Per Apple's HealthKit rules:

- We will **never share** your HealthKit data with third parties for advertising or marketing
- We will **never sell** your HealthKit data
- We will **never use** your HealthKit data outside of providing you the RunMana service
- We will **never write false data** to HealthKit
- Our use of HealthKit data is governed by Apple's policies in addition to this Privacy Policy
- You can revoke our access to HealthKit at any time: iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → RunMana

## 4. Where your data lives

| Data | Where it's stored | Encryption |
|------|-------------------|------------|
| Apple Health raw data (heart rate samples, GPS routes, etc.) | **On your iPhone only** | iOS encrypts on-device automatically |
| Your account, profile, training plan, race results, feedback | Supabase (Postgres database in the United States) | AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit |
| Your authentication tokens | Mac/iOS Keychain on your device | OS-level encryption |

## 5. Information sharing

**We do not share your information with third parties.** No advertisers. No data brokers. No partners. No researchers (unless you separately opt into a specific research program, which we may offer in the future with separate consent).

Exceptions where we may disclose information:

- **If required by law** — e.g., a valid subpoena. We would notify you unless legally prohibited from doing so.
- **To protect rights and safety** — e.g., if we have a good-faith reason to believe disclosure is necessary to protect someone's safety.
- **In a business transfer** — if Mana Athletic LLC is acquired or merges with another company, your data may transfer to the successor entity, who will be bound by this policy.

## 6. Your rights

You can, at any time:

- **Access** your data — see everything we have about you in the app
- **Export** your data — download a complete copy in JSON format (Settings → Export My Data)
- **Delete** your account and all your data — Settings → Delete My Account (this is irreversible)
- **Revoke HealthKit access** — iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → RunMana
- **Stop using the app** — at any time, for any reason, with no questions asked

If you're in the EU, California, or another jurisdiction with specific data rights laws, those rights also apply to you. Contact us at the email above to exercise them.

---

## 7. Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data is removed from our servers within 30 days. Backup copies (retained for disaster recovery) are purged within 90 days.

If you stop using the app but don't delete your account, your data remains stored so you can return later. If you'd like us to delete data from an inactive account, contact us.

---

## 8. Future research and the Collective Intelligence Layer

In the future, we may offer an **opt-in** research program where athletes can choose to contribute anonymized, aggregated training data to scientific research and methodology improvement. This will be:

- **Opt-in only** — never automatic, never default-on
- **Separately consented** — a clear, specific consent flow
- **Anonymized** — no personally identifying information transmitted
- **Aggregated** — research uses cohort-level statistics, not individual data
- **Revocable** — you can opt back out anytime

Until that program exists, no data is used for any purpose beyond providing you the service.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices:

- TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest in our database
- Row-Level Security so that one user can never access another user's data, even if our application code had a bug
- No third-party tracking SDKs in the app
- iOS automatic encryption for on-device data

No security is perfect. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we will notify you promptly and explain what happened, what data was affected, and what we're doing about it.

## 10. Children's privacy

RunMana is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

For high school athletes (typically 14–18), parental awareness is encouraged. The app is designed to be usable safely by HS-age athletes, but parents are welcome to review this policy with their child.
